# AWS Summit Sydney 2026

## Event Overview

AWS Summit Sydney 2026 is a free two-day event held on Tuesday the 13th and Wednesday the 14th of May 2026 at the International Convention Centre, also known as ICC Sydney, in Darling Harbour, Sydney, Australia.

The summit covers the latest in cloud and AI innovation, from agentic AI to serverless computing. Attendees can engage with industry leaders, AWS experts, interactive workshops, and customer showcases.

## Day Themes

Day 1 on Tuesday the 13th of May is Builders Day, with the theme Experiment, Build, Operate. It is designed for developers, engineers, solutions architects, and IT professionals who want hands-on technical learning.

Day 2 on Wednesday the 14th of May is Innovation Day, with the theme Create, Solve, Invent. It is designed for business leaders and technologists exploring digital transformation and strategic innovation.

## What to Expect

The summit features over 150 sessions across a wide range of topics including artificial intelligence, machine learning, agentic AI, serverless computing, security, data analytics, and more.

Key experiences include the AWS Village, an interactive zone where you can explore AWS services hands-on. There is also an AWS for Industries Zone, Training and Certification programs, and plenty of networking opportunities throughout both days.

## Voice AI Demo at the Summit

We are showcasing a live real-time voice AI agent demo at the AWS booth. The demo title is Building Real-time Voice Agents Using Deepgram, Pipecat and AWS.

This demo shows a cascaded voice agent architecture running entirely on AWS. Your speech is transcribed by Deepgram Nova 3 in real time. An LLM on Amazon Bedrock reasons about your question and generates a response. Deepgram Aura converts that response into natural sounding speech. Pipecat by Daily orchestrates the full pipeline end to end. Tavus renders the visual avatar you see on screen.

You are talking to this demo right now.
