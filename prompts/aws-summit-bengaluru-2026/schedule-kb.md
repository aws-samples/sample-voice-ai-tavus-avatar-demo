# AWS Summit Bengaluru 2026

## Event Overview

AWS Summit Bengaluru 2026 is a free two-day event held on Wednesday the 22nd and Thursday the 23rd of April 2026 at the KTPO Exhibition Center in Whitefield, Bengaluru, India.

The summit covers the latest in cloud and AI innovation, from agentic AI to serverless computing. Attendees can engage with industry leaders, AWS experts, interactive workshops, and customer showcases.

## Day Structure

Day 1 on Wednesday the 22nd of April is the Innovators Edition, designed for business leaders, CXOs, CTOs, and decision-makers focused on organisational transformation through cloud technologies.

Day 2 on Thursday the 23rd of April is the Technical Edition, designed for developers, engineers, and technical practitioners seeking hands-on experience with AWS services.

## Key Topics

The summit focuses on agentic and generative AI, data and compute services, cloud modernisation and enterprise transformation, AI agent development and deployment, and security and responsible AI practices.

## What to Expect

The summit features over 150 sessions across a wide range of topics. Key experiences include the AWS Village, an interactive zone where you can explore AWS services hands-on. There is also an AWS for Industries Zone, a Developer Community Zone, an 8-bit Agents Activation experience, a Startup Zone, Training and Certification programs, and networking opportunities throughout both days.

## Voice AI Demo at the Summit

We are showcasing a live real-time voice AI agent demo at the AWS booth. The demo title is Building Real-time Voice Agents Using Deepgram, Pipecat and AWS.

This demo shows a cascaded voice agent architecture running entirely on AWS. Your speech is transcribed by Deepgram Nova 3 in real time. An LLM on Amazon Bedrock reasons about your question and generates a response. Deepgram Aura converts that response into natural sounding speech. Pipecat by Daily orchestrates the full pipeline end to end. Tavus renders the visual avatar you see on screen.

You are talking to this demo right now.
